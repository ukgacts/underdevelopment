Biography

Crucial Todd, an enigmatic and mysterious figure hailing from the South Coast of the UK, has been a prominent figure in the multi-genre dance music scene for some years. However, in a bold move to shed any possibility of any DJ ego and concentrate on making the messages within his music the focal point, he rebranded himself under a new name that only he knows the meaning of. With a deep sense of responsibility to elevate the awareness and spirit of his audience, Crucial Todd constructs his unique brand of music - blending Jackin House, NUKG and DnB, which he aptly refers to as Jackin Bass.

Exclusively signed to the legendary Payback Project label in August 2023, Crucial Todd already has an impressive array of releases under his belt and with an ever- increasing arsenal of hits in the pipeline. Already getting support from BBC Introducing, Kiss FM and a whole array of independent radio stations, his music, characterised by his quirky approach, jackin beats, heavy bass and thought- provoking lyrics, is infused with positive messages that aim to not only make people dance but to open people up to their true potential.

Even the label Crucial Todd runs with SuperStyle Deluxe - The Payback Project has a mission to raise vibrations and help those that need it and have already been working with a company that go out and help clear overgrown gardens of people that are struggling for various reasons, as this brings joy to those that are being helped and those that watch the videos of the Payback team helping people that need it.

Crucial Todd and The Payback Project are ones to watch in 2025!
