About

We are a traditional narrowboat painting and signwriting firm based at the historic Knighton Wharf on the Shropshire Union Canal.

Since 2015, Lost Art Painters has specialised in authentic canal boat decoration, combining heritage craftsmanship with modern creativity.

Our aim isn’t to reinvent the wheel — it’s to preserve the timeless art of canal boat painting while helping you bring your ideas to life. Whether you’re restoring a historic narrowboat or adding a personal touch to your floating home, we work with you to turn a spark of inspiration into a beautifully hand- painted boat.

We take pride in traditional techniques, fine materials, and attention to detail — delivering a finish that exceeds expectations and celebrates the rich heritage of Britain’s waterways.

Our Story

Lost Art Painters is owned and run by Dan Holmes, who has an extensive background in art and design and holds a First-Class Honours Degree in Fine Art.

The company name came about when Dan taught himself how to paint beside the canal. With hours of trial, error, and experimentation — and few references to guide him — he uncovered what truly felt like a lost art: the traditional skill of coach painting narrowboats.

Before long, other boat owners began asking Dan to paint their vessels, and what started as a passion became a full-time craft. Today, the workshop at Knighton Wharf continues that same tradition — creating hand-painted narrowboats that blend heritage, design, and individuality.

Services
We offer a range of traditional narrowboat painting services, including:
Full and partial boat repaints
From full hull repaints to cabin tops, panels, and decorative detailing, we ensure a smooth, durable finish that reflects the pride you have in your boat.
Signwriting and decorative lettering
Using hand-painted techniques — never vinyl — we can create everything from classic name panels and scrollwork to modern styles that still respect the boat’s heritage.
Roses and castles artwork
We offer custom-painted roses, garlands, castles, and scenic panels, tailored to your colour scheme or style preference.
Coach lines, scrollwork, and detailing
We hand-paint coach lines, scrolls, corners, and border designs, using fine brushes and steady craftsmanship to achieve sharp, clean lines that highlight your boat’s shape and structure.
Restoration and repainting of historic narrowboats
We take great pride in restoring traditional narrowboats and historic working boats, respecting the methods and materials of the original builders.
Custom designs and bespoke commissions
We love projects that mix heritage and creativity, producing something that’s uniquely yours while rooted in traditional canal painting values.
